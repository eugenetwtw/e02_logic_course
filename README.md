# Introduction to Logic Course

Welcome to the "Introduction to Logic" course! Based on Irving M. Copi's "Introduction to Logic," this course is designed for middle school students to introduce basic concepts and applications of logic, helping students develop logical thinking and critical thinking skills. The course spans five days, covering logic fundamentals, argument structure, logical fallacies, deductive and inductive reasoning, and more.

## Course Overview

The course includes the following main content:
- **Day 1: Logic Basics and Language** - Introduction to the definition and importance of Logic, learning to identify Statements, and recognizing Premises and Conclusions in Arguments.
- **Day 2: Arguments and Fallacies** - Exploring Valid Arguments and Invalid Arguments, as well as common Logical Fallacies.
- **Day 3: Logical Connectives and Compound Statements** - Learning about Connectives, Negation, Conjunction, Disjunction, and other concepts, as well as Compound Statements.
- **Day 4: Deductive Reasoning and Logical Rules** - In-depth understanding of Deductive Reasoning, Syllogism, and their applications.
- **Day 5: Inductive Reasoning and Critical Thinking** - Exploring Inductive Reasoning, Argument by Analogy, and Critical Thinking techniques.

# 邏輯學入門課程

歡迎來到《邏輯學入門》課程！本課程基於Irving M. Copi的《邏輯學入門》，專為國中生設計，旨在介紹邏輯學的基本概念與應用，幫助學生培養邏輯思維與批判性思考能力。課程為期五天，涵蓋邏輯學基礎、論證結構、邏輯謬誤、演繹與歸納推理等主題。

## 課程概述

本課程包含以下主要內容：
- **第一天：邏輯學基礎與語言** - 介紹邏輯學 (Logic) 的定義與重要性，學習陳述句 (Statement) 的辨識，以及論證 (Argument) 中的前提 (Premise) 與結論 (Conclusion)。
- **第二天：論證與謬誤** - 探討有效論證 (Valid Argument) 與無效論證 (Invalid Argument)，以及常見的邏輯謬誤 (Fallacy)。
- **第三天：邏輯連接詞與複合陳述句** - 學習連接詞 (Connective)、否定 (Negation)、連接 (Conjunction)、選言 (Disjunction) 等概念，以及複合陳述句 (Compound Statement)。
- **第四天：演繹推理與邏輯規則** - 深入了解演繹推理 (Deductive Reasoning)、三段論 (Syllogism) 及其應用。
- **第五天：歸納推理與批判性思考** - 探討歸納推理 (Inductive Reasoning)、類比推理 (Argument by Analogy)，以及批判性思考 (Critical Thinking) 的技巧。
